SolSolum LLC ("we", "us", or "our") built DebutK: K-POP ME! as a free-to-play mobile game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. DebutK: K-POP ME! does not require an account, login, or any personal details to play.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of children's data being stored or shared.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect any data, we have no data to retain or delete. Data stored locally on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.
